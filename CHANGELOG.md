## [Unreleased] — v0.1 (manifesto)

### Added
- `docs/COMMERCIAL.md` — the honest business case. TAM/SAM/SOM, unit economics, 3 revenue scenarios, 10 risks ranked by severity, the single metric that decides the outcome (Section adoption at 30/60/90 days).
- `docs/USER-WORKFLOW.md` — the deep companion to the PRD. Every screen, every state, every transition, every edge case. 1,700+ lines covering 20 sections.
- `PHILOSOPHY.md` — the deepest layer. The why underneath the why.
- Initial architecture, manifesto, and product spec
- Three-mechanism framework (Craving Machine, Infinite Game, Invisible Scoreboard) — applied to product features
- Privacy architecture with structural data minimization
- Localization plan covering 8 day-one-to-day-90 locales
- Anti-PBL design checklist
- Anti-metric instrumentation plan
- Issue templates (bug, feature, design) with mechanism and privacy checks
- Pull request template with mechanism, privacy, and localization checks
- Monorepo scaffold placeholder (`apps/`, `packages/`)

### Notes
- v0.1 is the **manifesto and architecture**. No product code yet.
- Phase 1 (monorepo scaffold) is the next milestone — see `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md` §"Build phases"
